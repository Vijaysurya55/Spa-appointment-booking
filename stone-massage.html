Stone Massage Therapy

Stone massage therapy involves the use of heated stones to apply pressure and heat to the body. This technique helps to relax muscles and allows the therapist to apply deeper pressure if needed.

At Mystical Salon & Spa, we offer a luxurious stone massage experience using smooth, heated stones. The warmth of the stones helps to loosen tense muscles and allows for a deeper massage without increased pressure.

Treat yourself to the soothing and revitalizing experience of stone massage therapy. Book your appointment now and let the healing power of warm stones enhance your relaxation and rejuvenation.
